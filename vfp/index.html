About the Band
Rock for All Occasions - Puget Sound and throughout the NW
Vote for Pedro the Band is one of Puget Sound's hottest cover and party bands. Comprised of seasoned musicians with years of professional experience under their belts, VFP can bring a high-energy dance party to major events like corporate or wedding events, or a small intimate acoustic evening for your house birthday party. They've played outdoor parks, block parties, casinos, ski areas, and just about everything in-between. No matter what your need, if you're looking for fun, high-quality, professional rock'n'roll for your next event, check out Vote for Pedro the Band!
