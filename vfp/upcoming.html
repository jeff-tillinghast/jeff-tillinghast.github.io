Where Will Pedro Be Next?

As one of Puget Sound's premiere cover and event bands, Vote for Pedro has played clubs, casinos, and venues throughout the region. You might have seen VFP at places like:

  • 7 Cedars Casino, Sequim
  • The Lime, Kirkland
  • The Brick, Roslyn
  • Fado, Pioneer Square
  • The Alaskan, Juneau
  • Murphy's, Wallingford
  • Wilde Rover, Kirkland
  • H20, Anacortes
  • Finn McCool's, University District
  • Rock Bottom Brewery, Belleuve
  • World of Beer, Renton
  • Roosevelt Alehouse, Seattle
  • J&M Cafe, Pioneer Square
  • Kate's Pub, Wallingford
  • Zombiefest Northwest
  • Kirkland Summerfest
  • Wallingford Wurst Festival
  • City of Seattle Rainier Beach and University District Block Parties

Vote for Pedro has played corporate events, weddings, and other private parties throughout the PNW, including travel to Montana, Colorado, and Alaska. If you are looking for high-quality and professional live music for your venue or upcoming event, get in touch through the Booking Inquiry form.
